Tetris

Tetris is a classic puzzle video game enjoyed by players of all ages.

The game challenges players to arrange falling blocks called Tetrominoes to form complete horizontal lines. As the game progresses, the speed increases, making it more challenging and exciting. Tetris helps improve problem-solving skills, focus, and quick decision-making.

Slither.io

Slither.io is not the original Snake game, but it shares similar features and brings back memories of the classic Nokia games we played as kids.

In Slither.io, players control a snake, eat glowing pellets to grow longer, and compete against others to become the biggest on the map. Its simple controls and fast-paced multiplayer gameplay make it fun, addictive, and challenging.
