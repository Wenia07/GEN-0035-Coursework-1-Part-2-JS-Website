About Us

Our aim is to create a global platform where players can connect, compete, and stay updated through live match scores and detailed player information. We currently focus on classic pixel-style games while also showcasing global competitions, such as CSGO tournaments, giving players access to real-time match updates from around the world.

Players can participate in 1 vs 1 matches by selecting games from our Match page. In addition to competitive features, our platform allows users to add other registered players, making it easy to build friendships and connect with gamers internationally. This social feature encourages interaction beyond matches and helps create a strong global gaming community.

We also provide comprehensive information about the games available on our website, including gameplay details, controls, and game history. This helps both new and experienced players learn more about each game and improve their skills.

Currently, our website is in beta, as we continue to develop and expand our platform. More games are in progress and will be added in the future as we work to enhance the overall experience for our users.
