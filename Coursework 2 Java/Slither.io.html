Slither.io

About Us

Slither.io is a massively multiplayer browser game developed by Steve Howse. Players control a snake-like avatar that grows by consuming multicolored pellets, either from other players or that naturally appear on the map. The game is similar to Agar.io and reminds players of the classic arcade game Snake. Its popularity increased after being promoted by YouTubers such as PewDiePie. An Android version was released on March 27, 2016, and quickly topped the Google Play Store charts.

Objective

The main goal of Slither.io is to grow your snake as long as possible while avoiding collisions with other players. Consuming pellets and outmaneuvering opponents are key strategies to become the longest snake on the server.

General Gameplay Info

  • GOAL - Control your snake and grow as long as possible by consuming pellets and defeating other players.
  • PELLETS - Eat multicolored pellets that spawn naturally or come from defeated snakes to increase your mass and length.
  • BOOST MODE - Press space or click to speed up your snake. Boosting loses some mass but helps outmaneuver opponents.
  • DEFEATING OTHERS - Trap other snakes by coiling around them to force collisions. Their bodies turn into bright pellets matching their color for you to consume.
  • AVOID COLLISIONS - Hitting another snakeâ€™s body or the border ends the game. Only collisions with other snakes create consumable pellets.
  • STRATEGY - Use skillful movement, coiling, and boost maneuvers to outplay opponents.
  • VICTORY MESSAGE - The player with the biggest snake at the end can send a "victory message" to the world.

Controls & Scores

  • CONTROLS (Browser) - Move your snake using the mouse or arrow keys.
  • CONTROLS (Mobile) - Press and hold in the direction you want to move. Optionally, enable thumbstick mode for easier control.
  • SPEED BOOST - Click/hold mouse or press up arrow/space to boost. Boosting sheds some length but helps outmaneuver opponents.
